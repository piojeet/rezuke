Have a look at my most frequently asked questions.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes, we offer a variety of discounts throughout the year. Sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest promotions and special offers. Additionally, we provide discounts for bulk service purchases and long-term contracts. Contact our sales team for more information.

I’m looking for different service

We understand that every business has unique needs. If you don’t see the specific service you’re looking for, please reach out to us. Our team is flexible and experienced in a wide range of services, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your requirements or direct you to a trusted partner who can.

How many hours in monthly package?

Our monthly packages are designed to be flexible to meet your business needs. Typically, our standard package includes 20 hours of service per month. However, we also offer customizable packages that can be tailored to include more or fewer hours based on your specific requirements. Contact us to discuss the best option for your business.

Do you offer combo packages?

Yes, we offer combo packages that combine multiple services at a discounted rate. Our combo packages are designed to provide comprehensive solutions for businesses looking to streamline their operations and maximize efficiency. Whether you need a mix of consulting, design, and development services, we can create a package that meets your needs. Reach out to us to learn more about our available combo packages.
